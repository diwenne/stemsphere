Our Mission

Stemsphere Foundation is dedicated to providing STEM education and mentorship for young people to become problem-solvers, leaders, and innovators. Our vision involves a world where young people are empowered to unleash their creativity in STEM and shape a better future for all.

Workshop Content

Workshops offered by Stemsphere Foundation have a variety of structures. Here's what a typical workshop includes:

  • A fun and engaging hands-on project that reflects the main topic discussed
  • Various group activities that give students realistic examples of STEM and its connection to everyday life
  • A final discussion and reflection to reinforce learning and reflect on the topic
